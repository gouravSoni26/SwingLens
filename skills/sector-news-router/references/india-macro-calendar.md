# India Macro Event Calendar

Recurring events that affect NSE equity markets. Use this to add time-sensitivity to news context in Groq briefs.

---

## Monthly Events

| Date (approx) | Event | Sectors Most Affected |
|---------------|-------|-----------------------|
| 1st of month | SIAM/FADA auto sales data | Auto, Auto Ancillary |
| 1st of month | PMI Manufacturing & Services | Broad market |
| 7th–10th | RBI policy statement (if MPC month) | Banking, NBFC, Rate-sensitives |
| Mid-month | CPI inflation data | FMCG, Banking, RBI-sensitive |
| Mid-month | WPI inflation data | Metals, FMCG, Cement |
| Mid-month | IIP (Industrial Production) | Industrials, Cement, Metals |
| Last week | FII/DII flow data (monthly summary) | Broad market |

---

## RBI MPC Schedule (Bi-monthly)

MPC meets approximately every 2 months. Typical months: **February, April, June, August, October, December**.

Key outputs: Repo rate decision, stance (accommodative/neutral/withdrawal), GDP/inflation projections.

**Impact:** Rate hike = negative for Banking (NIM pressure short-term), Real Estate, Auto. Rate cut = positive for all rate-sensitives.

## Annual Events

| Month | Event | Market Impact |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| February 1 | Union Budget | Broad market — sector allocation shifts |
| March–April | FY end (March 31) | Institutional rebalancing, tax-loss harvesting |
| June–September | Monsoon season | FMCG (rural demand), Cement (construction slow), Agri |
| October–November | Festive season (Navratri, Diwali) | Auto, FMCG, Consumer Durables, Retail |
| December–January | FII year-end positioning | Broad market, large caps |
